Events


The CS Club partakes in various events throughout the year. These can include regional/state competitions, field trips, fundraisers, and workshops.

In addition, Alhambra CS Club hosts computer science events of its own. Through these events, you are able to learn and get firsthand experience on elements of computer science. If you're advanced enough, you may be eligible to be a volunteer to help out at these events for community service hours. These can count toward CSHS hours, graduation requirements, or any other organization such as NHS and CSF.

These events include:

SkillsUSA
Summer CyberCamps
Cal Poly's CCIC

Hour of Code
MJHS Field Trip
Holidary Frolic

...and much more!
